ABOUT
PHOTOGRAPHER

Juozas Kazlauskas (1941-2002) was a famous Lithuanian photographer, who, among other themes, has a broad portfolio of documentary works on political topics, research expeditions and travel photography. The geography of his works varies from places like pre-war Chechnya, Chernobyl after the disaster or Lithuania at it's dawn of independance, to the Arctic, Siberia and more.

Photographer Juozas Kazlauskas has left a substantial archive of authentic vintage, autographed prints as well as negatives, which is now being managed by the Juozas Kazlauskas Foundation.

JUOZAS KAZLAUSKAS
FOUNDATION

Juozas Kazlauskas Foundation based in Vilnius, Lithuania is a non-profit organization that archives, researches and exhibits photographer Juozas Kazlauskas’ works.

We are currently looking for exhibition spaces and partners for collaboration, as well as potential print buyers.

The copyrights to Juozas Kazlauskas’s photography belongs to Dalia Kazlauskienė, she is also a director of the foundation.
