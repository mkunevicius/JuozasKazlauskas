ABOUT THE
PHOTOGRAPHER

Juozas Kazlauskas (1941-2002) was a famous Lithuanian photographer who among other themes has a broad portfolio of documentary works on critical political topics, research expeditions as well as travel photography. The geography of his works varies from pre-war Chechnya, Chernobyl after the disaster or Lithuania at its dawn of independance, to the Arctic, Siberia and more.

Kazlauskas has left a substantial archive of authentic vintage, autographed prints as well as negatives, which is now being managed by the Photographer Juozas Kazlauskas Charity and Support Fund (Juozas Kazlauskas Fund).

JUOZAS KAZLAUSKAS
FUND

We are based in Vilnius, Lithuania. The Fund is a non-profit organization that archives, researches and exhibits Juozas Kazlauskas’ works.

We are currently looking for exhibition spaces and partners for collaboration, as well as potential print buyers.

The copyrights to Juozas Kazlauskas’s photography belongs to Dalia Kazlauskienė, who also supervises the Fund.
